My Top Three Movies of All Time

Star Trek 2009

Star Trek 2009 is a moving cinematic experience with exceptional performances from Chris Pine, Zachery Quinto, and Karl Urban.

V for Vendetta 2005

V for Vendetta is an inspiring film about the fight to regain lost freedoms. The cinematograpy plays a major role in the storytelling.There are no wasted scenes in this movie.

Amazing Grace 2006

Amazing Grace is a biographical drama film about the abolitionist campaign against the slave trade in the British Empire led by William Wilberforce.
